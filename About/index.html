About

Hello! My name is Margo, and I’m a baker with a deep passion for creating custom cakes that not only look stylish but taste incredible too. Baking has always been a true love of mine, and over the years, it has become much more than just a hobby — it’s my way of bringing joy to people through beautifully crafted, delicious cakes.

While I have a solid foundation in classic desserts, my experience extends to a wide range of international cuisines. Whether it’s the intricate flavours of French patisserie, the delicate textures of Italian pastries, I’ve developed a diverse skill set that allows me to create something truly special for every occasion.

I believe that every cake should tell a story, and I work closely with my clients to design bespoke cakes that reflect their personal style and vision. From weddings and birthdays to corporate events, I take pride in delivering cakes that are as unique as the people who order them.

Thank you for stopping by — I can’t wait to work with you to create a cake that’s not only visually stunning but also unforgettable in taste.
