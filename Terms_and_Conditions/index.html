Deposits and Payments

  • To secure your order and the agreed pick-up date, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking.

Delivery and Pick-Up

  • Orders can be picked up at a pre-agreed time. Please ensure you arrive on time.
  • Delivery is available to Huntingdon, Peterborough, Cambridge, and nearby areas. Delivery fees are assessed individually based on location and time.

Cancellations and Refunds
