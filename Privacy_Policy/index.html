When placing orders or contacting us, we may collect the following personal data:

We use analytical tools to collect non-personal information about your interaction with our Site, such as:

The personal data collected is used to:

We use usage data to:

We process your data under the following legal bases:

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at dreamycake.info@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to ensure your personal data is protected against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.
